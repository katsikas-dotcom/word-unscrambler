Words with Z

Every valid Scrabble Z word — from ZA to PIZZAZZ — sorted by length

Z is worth 10 points — tied with Q for the highest letter value in Scrabble. Unlike Q, Z is much easier to play because it combines freely with common vowels. ZA (pizza, informal) is a legal 2-letter word worth 11 points — one of the most important Scrabble words to know.

Z Strategy Guide

Z is more versatile than Q because it doesn't require specific letter combinations. Here's how to maximize it:

  • ZA and ZAX should be your go-to short plays when you need to dump Z fast
  • Double-Z words (JAZZ, BUZZ, FIZZ) on a Triple Letter Score = massive points
  • JAZZ on a Triple Word Score with J on a Double Letter: 87+ points
  • ZEPHYR and ZIGZAG are great rack-clearers that use 6 tiles
  • ZEN, ZAP, ZAG are useful 3-letter connectors for tight boards
  • PIZZAZZ (47 points base) is the highest-scoring Z word if you can ever play it
