Why 2-Letter Words Are Essential

Expert Scrabble players estimate that knowing 2-letter words is worth 50–100 extra points per game. Here's why:

  • Parallel plays: Place a word next to an existing one so each pair of touching letters forms a legal 2-letter word — you score both words
  • Hooks: Extend any word by one letter when you can form a valid 2-letter word at the junction
  • Rescue plays: Even the worst rack of letters usually contains at least one 2-letter combo you can play
  • Board opening: Playing short words tactically keeps premium squares accessible for you later

The Most Useful 2-Letter Words

The highest-value: QI (11), ZA (11), ZAX (though 3 letters). Best vowel dumps: AA, AE, AI, OE, OI, OO. Best consonant plays: MM, SH, HM.

Memorization Tips

  • Learn the vowel pairs first: AA, AE, AI, OE, OI, OO
  • Memorize J, Q, X, Z words — they're rare and valuable
  • Practice with flashcard apps: AnkiApp works well
  • Note words ONLY in TWL vs. those also in SOWPODS
  • Use them in casual play to build muscle memory
