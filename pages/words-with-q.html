Q Strategy for Scrabble

Q is the most feared tile in Scrabble because players don't know what to do when they have it. Here's how experts handle it:

  • Don't panic — QI is always a valid 2-letter play (11 points)
  • Keep a U when you draw a Q — trade other tiles, not the U
  • If you must dump Q without U: QAT, QOPH, QANAT are your options
  • QUIZ on a Triple Word Score = 33 points minimum
  • QUARTZ on a double letter Z + triple word = 80+ points
