Words with X

From AX and EX to LUXE and OXIDE — every useful Scrabble X word, scored

X is worth 8 points — the third-highest tile value in Scrabble. Unlike Q, X has two legal 2-letter words (XI and XU) and combines readily with common vowels. Knowing your short X words and strategic placement patterns is worth 20–30 extra points per game.

X Placement Strategy

X is the most versatile high-value tile. Unlike Q, it doesn't require specific letters and combines in every direction:

  • Double Letter Score under X = 16 points just for the X tile, before the rest of the word
  • XI and XU as parallel plays: Place a word next to an existing one so XI or XU forms — easy 9–18 points
  • OX hook: Many words accept X as a hook (BOX from BO, FOX from FO, TAX from TA)
  • -EX words (HEX, SEX, LEX, REX, VEX) all score 10–14 points with only 3 tiles
  • Avoid hoarding X — unlike Z, there's rarely a "perfect" moment that's significantly better than playing it now
