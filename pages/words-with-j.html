Words with J

From JO and JAB to JUKEBOX — every Scrabble J word sorted by length & score

J is worth 8 points — tied with X as the third-highest tile in Scrabble. Unlike Q, J combines naturally with common vowels (A, O, U) and works well in short plays. There is only one legal 2-letter J word — JO — making short J words all the more important to memorize.

J Strategy for Scrabble

J is one of the easiest high-value tiles to play because it pairs freely with A, O, and U — the three most common vowels:

  • JO is your only 2-letter dump — memorize it, use it freely
  • JAZZ (29pts) is the highest-scoring common 4-letter word in Scrabble
  • JAZZY (33pts) scores more than most 7-letter words without a premium square
  • J before A words (JAB, JAM, JAW, JAY, JAR) are the most common plays — A is drawn more than any vowel
  • On a Double Letter Score under J, even JO scores 17 points
  • JINX (18pts) combines J with X for a devastating combo — look for it
