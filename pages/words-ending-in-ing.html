Words Ending in -ING

From RING to BLAZING — every useful -ING word for Scrabble and word games

The suffix -ING is the most powerful pattern in Scrabble. It converts almost any verb into a valid word, and 7-letter -ING words frequently qualify for the 50-point bingo bonus. This page lists the most useful -ING words sorted by length and Scrabble score.

Why -ING Words Win Games

The -ING suffix is a Scrabble superweapon for three reasons:

  • Bingo bait: Keeping I, N, G on your rack means one more tile + a 4-letter word = a 7-tile bingo (50 bonus points)
  • Hook plays: Many nouns and adjectives accept -ING as a suffix, doubling your extension options
  • High consonant value: G scores 2 points, so an -ING ending adds 4 base points to any root word
  • Board flexibility: -ING words can extend leftward from any existing word ending in a compatible letter

Bingo-Worthy -ING Combos to Practice

Keep ING + any 4 of: A E R S T on your rack. Words like STARING, STRANGE, SEATING, EARNING, RESTING are some of the most common 7-letter plays in tournament Scrabble.
