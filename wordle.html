How to Use the Wordle Solver

The Wordle Solver uses your clue history to filter a list of 2,000+ five-letter words down to only those that match all your constraints.

  1. Type your first guess (e.g. CRANE) into the first row
  2. Click the color buttons below each letter to match Wordle's feedback
  3. Add more guess rows as you play
  4. Hit Find Answers to see every word that fits all clues
  5. The top picks are highlighted — choose one with new letters to maximize info

Best opening words: CRANE, SLATE, TRACE, STARE, ROAST, LATER, AROSE — these cover the most common English letters (E, T, A, O, I, N, S, R).
