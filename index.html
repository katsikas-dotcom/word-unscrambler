How to Use the Word Unscrambler

Our free tool searches a dictionary of 3,000+ common English words to find every valid word hidden in your scrambled letters in under a second.

Step-by-step

  • Type your scrambled letters into the input box (up to 15 letters)
  • Use ? for blank Scrabble tiles
  • Optionally filter by starting letter, ending, or required substring
  • Click any word chip to see its definition and Scrabble point value
  • Results are sorted longest → shortest, then by point value within each length

Why use a word unscrambler?

  • Stuck in Scrabble or Words with Friends? Find the highest-scoring play
  • Solving crossword clues with known letters
  • Teaching vocabulary and letter patterns to kids
  • Brain training and mental exercise
